Walking Robot

Progress Update

The robot is able to slowly walk,
however, it can only do so by being assisted.
As seen in the picture, the primary material is cardboard.
While strong and light, it is not enough to carry the weight of
the arduino and the breadboard on top. In around two weeks,
I will have access to a 3D printing shop, and I will use that to
create a more durable structure for the robot. I will keep this
website updated.
